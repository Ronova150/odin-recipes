Macaroni and Cheese; Gluten Free

Ingridients

  • 1 pkg. Mac-cheese
  • 2 Tbsp. of Milk
  • 3 Tbsp. of margarine or butter
  • 6 cups of water

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add the pasta and Cook for 8 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally
  3. Drain the pasta and returned to the pan
  4. Add all Ingridients and Stir together
