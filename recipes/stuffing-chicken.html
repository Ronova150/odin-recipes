Stuffing Mix; Chicken

Ingredients

  • The box of Stuffing Mix
  • 1 1/2 Cups of Waters
  • 1/4 of butter or margarine

Steps

Stovetop Directions

  1. Heat 1 1/2 cups water and 1/2 cup butter or margarine to boil
  2. Stir in content of stuffing mix pounch
  3. remove from heat, and cover, let stann 5 minutes
  4. Fluff with a Fork and serve

Microwave Directions

  1. Mix 1 1/2 cups hot water, 1/4 cup butter 1 1/2 quart Microwave safe bowl.
  2. Cover and microwave on HIGH 5 to 6 minutes
  3. Fluff with fork and serve
